## ⚠️ FACES Critical Clauses

### Clause 5 - No modification without prior approval

FACES objects must be used "in the form they are delivered." Resizing, cropping, format conversion to `.webp`, and neutral renaming all require **prior written agreement** from the FACES Technical Agent.

**Action:** Email `facesdatabase@mpib-berlin.mpg.de` with the signed agreement and request pre-approval for:
- Resizing images for responsive web display
- Converting to `.webp` format for mobile performance
- Renaming files to neutral identifiers (no age/metadata in public filenames)

### Clause 4 - Online experiment disclaimer

For web-based experiments, participants must agree to a mandatory disclaimer:
> "I herewith commit myself not to distribute, publish, copy, or further disseminate to third parties any of the objects shown to me in the following."

**Action:** Add this disclaimer as a required consent step in the frontend before any FACES images are shown.

### Clause 6 - No publication of images

FACES images cannot appear in papers, posters, or presentations. Only 6 exempt persons (004, 066, 079, 116, 140, 168) may be published, and only for illustrating methodology.
